Software Design Narrative

This artifact is redesigning my travlr website from my full stack dev course to be a platform for TCG events. I created this course website a month ago in the full stack dev course. I chose to work on this because I think it will demonstrate my abilities and competency in full-stack development.

For this enhancement I had to do a full refactor of my codebase (there are still some lingering artifacts of the old codebase that will be addressed in the future). This took quite a long time, and this was on top of having to design a new database schema that meets the needs for my new application.

This enhancement taught me a lot about the time loss involved in a refactor. Based on what I’ve had to do with such a small codebase, I will be sure to apply this to my projects going forward, and dropping the mentality I used to have, which was get a small proof of concept up and running asap at any cost. Now that I know just how bad the time investment is in even the little things like renaming variables or files across a codebase, I will be sure to properly plan out my projects ahead of time, and do my best to future proof them (and design schemas and such with future needs in mind).
